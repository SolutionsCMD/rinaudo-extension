Privacy Policy

Rinaudo Capital (browser extension) · Last updated 25 June 2026

This policy explains what the Rinaudo Capital browser extension ("the extension") does with your data. It applies only to the extension, not to the rinaudoglobal.com website or any other service.

What the extension does

The extension lets you earn tickets in Mizkif's game by engaging with his content — liking, commenting on, and watching his promoted posts and videos on X, YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, and voting on live polls from his Kick stream — and it notifies you when he goes live or posts. Tickets are credited to the account you link once with "Connect with Kick."

What we store

  • Your account token. When you choose "Connect with Kick," a single authentication token is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local). It is used only to authenticate your requests to s2.jsolutions.dev, never leaves your browser except in the Authorization header of calls to that server, and is removed when you disconnect or uninstall.
  • Local state only. The extension also keeps small bookkeeping values locally (e.g. the last poll/video/post already shown, so you aren't notified twice, and where you dragged the on-page card). These stay on your device.

What we send, and to whom

The extension communicates with only two servers:

  • https://s2.jsolutions.dev (the game API): to exchange your Connect-with-Kick login for an account token; to record when you like, comment on, or watch one of Mizkif's promoted posts or videos (we send the platform, the post/video ID, and the action — for watching, periodic "still watching" signals — so the tickets can be credited); to fetch the active poll and submit your vote; and to fetch the public reward-rate list. We do not send the text of your comments — only that you commented on a given post.
  • https://rinaudoglobal.com (public status feed): the extension periodically checks whether Mizkif is live or has posted, to power notifications. This requires no login and sends none of your data.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or telemetry.
  • No advertising, and no selling or sharing of data with third parties.
  • No tracking of your general browsing. The extension runs only on Mizkif's Kick channel and on X, YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram post pages, and only to detect your deliberate engagement with his promoted posts and to show its own on-page card. It does not read or collect the contents of those pages (your comment draft is checked locally for length only, and is never transmitted).
  • No collection of names, emails, payment details, or other personal information by the extension itself.

Permissions, and why

  • identity — the one-time "Connect with Kick" sign-in.
  • storage — to store your account token and local state.
  • alarms — a periodic timer to check for open polls and new content.
  • notifications — desktop alerts when a poll opens or Mizkif goes live or posts.
  • host access to s2.jsolutions.dev and rinaudoglobal.com — to talk to the game API and the public notifications feed.
  • host access to kick.com — to show the vote card on Mizkif's stream.
  • host access to x.com, twitter.com, youtube.com, tiktok.com, and instagram.com — to detect and credit your likes, comments, and watches on Mizkif's promoted posts and videos there.

Your choices

You can disconnect at any time from the extension popup, which deletes the stored token and tells the server to revoke it. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.
