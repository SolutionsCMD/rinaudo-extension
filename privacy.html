Privacy Policy

Rinaudo Capital — Live Votes (browser extension) · Last updated 10 June 2026

This policy explains what the Rinaudo Capital — Live Votes browser extension ("the extension") does with your data. It applies only to the extension, not to the rinaudoglobal.com website or any other service.

What the extension does

The extension lets members vote on live stock picks from Mizkif's stream, notifies them when a poll opens or new content is posted, and credits watch-to-earn activity to their Rinaudo account.

What we store

  • Your account token. When you choose "Connect with Kick," a single authentication token is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local). It is used only to authenticate your requests to rinaudoglobal.com. It never leaves your browser except in the Authorization header of calls to rinaudoglobal.com, and it is removed when you disconnect.
  • Local state only. The extension also keeps small bookkeeping values locally (e.g. the last poll already shown, last-seen videos) so it doesn't notify you twice. These stay on your device.

What we send to rinaudoglobal.com

The extension communicates only with https://rinaudoglobal.com, to:

  • exchange your Connect-with-Kick login for an account token;
  • fetch the currently active poll or trade vote, and submit your vote;
  • check whether the stream is live or new videos/posts exist, to power notifications;
  • report watch-to-earn activity — the video ID and seconds watched — for eligible videos, so the activity can be credited to your account.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or telemetry.
  • No advertising, and no selling or sharing of data with third parties.
  • No tracking of your general browsing. The extension runs only on Mizkif's Kick channel page and on YouTube watch/shorts pages, and does not read or collect the contents of those pages.
  • No collection of names, emails, payment details, or other personal information by the extension itself.

Permissions, and why

  • identity — the one-time "Connect with Kick" sign-in.
  • storage — to store your account token locally.
  • alarms — a periodic timer to check for open polls and new content.
  • notifications — desktop alerts when a poll opens or new content is posted.
  • host access to kick.com, rinaudoglobal.com, and youtube.com — to show the vote card on the stream, talk to the Rinaudo APIs, and run watch-to-earn on eligible videos.

Your choices

You can disconnect at any time from the extension popup, which deletes the stored token and tells rinaudoglobal.com to revoke it. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.
